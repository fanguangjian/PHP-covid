Add Mint analytics tracking into your site's header and add Bird Feeder tracking to your feeds.

== Description ==

Lightweight plugin to:

1. enqueue <a href="http://haveamint.com">Mint</a> analytics tracking into your site's header
1. add Bird Feeder tracking to your feeds

== Installation ==

1. Upload `minty.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Clear cache (if applicable) and load your home page, make sure `/mint/?js` is included
1. Load up one of your feeds, make sure Bird Feeder caught it
